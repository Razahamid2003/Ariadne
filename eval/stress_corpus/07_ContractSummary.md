# SPECTRA-300 Contract Summary
Document: MDS-CON-300. Extract of key commercial terms (fictional).

## Clause 4 - Milestones and payment
- M1 (Clause 4.1) Contract award. Payment: 15 percent. Date: 6 January.
- M2 (Clause 4.2) PDR complete. Payment: 10 percent. Date: 31 January.
- M3 (Clause 4.3) CDR complete. Payment: 15 percent. Date: 31 March.
- M4 (Clause 4.4) First article acceptance. Payment: 25 percent. Date: 30 June. Status: AT RISK.
- M5 (Clause 4.5) Delivery of all units. Payment: 25 percent. Date: 30 September.
- M6 (Clause 4.6) Final acceptance and warranty start. Payment: 10 percent. Date: 31 October.

## Clause 7 - Delay and penalties
Clause 7.3: Liquidated damages of 0.5 percent of the milestone value apply for each
completed week of delay, capped at 8 percent of the total contract value.
Clause 7.4: Delays caused by customer-furnished equipment are excluded from penalties.

## Clause 9 - Warranty and support
Clause 9.1: Warranty is 24 months from final acceptance (M6).
Clause 9.2: Guaranteed spare-part availability is 7 years.
Clause 9.5: On-site response for critical faults is within 48 hours.

## Clause 11 - Intellectual property
Clause 11.2: Background IP remains with Meridian Defence Systems; foreground IP
created under the contract is jointly owned.

Note: M4 first article acceptance is at risk because acceptance retest of SR-022
depends on the backordered FA-12 filter (26-week lead time).
