# Customer FAQ

**Q: How long is the warranty on Helios products?**
The Atlas-7 carries a 36-month warranty. The Borealis-3 carries a 24-month warranty.

**Q: How do I request a repair (RMA)?**
Email support@heliosdynamics.example with your serial number. You will receive an
RMA number within two business days. Do not ship hardware without an RMA number.

**Q: What is the support response time?**
Standard support responds within 8 business hours. Priority support responds within
2 business hours.
