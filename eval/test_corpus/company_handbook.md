# Helios Dynamics — Company Handbook

Helios Dynamics was founded in 2014 and is headquartered in Austin, Texas.
The company employs 480 people across four offices.

The Chief Executive Officer is Dr. Marion Vale. The Chief Technology Officer
is Anil Raghavan.

## Leave policy
Full-time employees receive 22 days of paid time off (PTO) per year. PTO begins
accruing on the employee's start date. Unused PTO of up to 5 days may be carried
into the following year.

## Remote work
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Fully remote arrangements
require director approval.

## Mission
Helios Dynamics builds ruggedized sensing systems for harsh environments.
