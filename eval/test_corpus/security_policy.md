# Information Security Policy

## Passwords
Passwords must be at least 14 characters long and must be changed every 90 days.
Reusing any of the previous 10 passwords is prohibited.

## Data retention
Customer records are retained for 7 years, after which they are securely destroyed.

## Incident response
On discovering a suspected breach, staff must report it to the security team
within 1 hour. The security team acknowledges within 30 minutes and begins
containment. A written post-incident report is required within 5 business days.
